Upcoming Alpha Trending Features

Funnels

A service that obfuscates the trail of transactions. Similar to 'mixers' or 'tumblers', funnels will jumble up tokens with others before they reach their final destination, making the trail hard to follow.

Private Swaps

A decentralized exchange (DEX) feature that allows users to swap between different tokens directly from their wallet, without their transaction being publicly traceable.

Gift Cards

Prepaid blockchain-based cards that can be used for transactions. These could essentially act like regular gift cards, allowing for anonymous transactions as they disconnect the purchaser from the end user.

Stealth Transactions

Allow for optional stealth transactions where sender, receiver, and amount details are hidden from public view.

Anti-MEV Swaps

A revolutionary feature aimed at maintaining fairness in transaction execution.

Decentralized Registration

Allowing users to access and use the platform without the need togo through a detailed KYC process, thereby maintaining user privacy from the onset.
